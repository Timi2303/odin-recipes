Fried-Rice

list of ingredients for preparing Fried Rice:

  • Cooked rice (preferably cold or day-old rice)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Peas
  • Sweet corn
  • Eggs
  • Cooked chicken, shrimp, or any other protein (optional)
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce (optional)
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Green onions (scallions) for garnish

And here are the steps for cooking Fried Rice:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger to the hot oil. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add diced carrots, sliced green beans, peas, and sweet corn to the wok. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  4. If using cooked chicken, shrimp, or any other protein, add it to the wok and stir-fry until heated through.
  5. Push the vegetables and protein to one side of the wok, creating a space in the center. Crack the eggs into the space and scramble them until they are cooked.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the wok and stir-fry everything together, breaking up any clumps of rice with a spatula.
  7. Drizzle soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and a few drops of sesame oil over the rice. Sprinkle with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir-fry for a few more minutes to ensure the flavors are well combined and the rice is heated through.
  8. Remove the wok from the heat and garnish with sliced green onions (scallions).
  9. Your delicious Fried Rice is now ready to be served!

Enjoy your meal
