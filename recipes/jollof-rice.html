Jollof-Rice

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken or beef (optional)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Red bell pepper
  • Scotch bonnet peppers or habanero peppers
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Bay leaves
  • Thyme
  • Paprika or cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Curry powder
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Green peas (optional)
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Sweet corn (optional)
  • Green bell pepper (optional)

And here are the steps for cooking Jollof Rice:

  1. Blend the tomatoes, onions, red bell pepper, Scotch bonnet peppers, garlic, and ginger to make a smooth puree.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot or saucepan.
  3. Add the blended puree to the hot oil and cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the puree thickens and the excess water evaporates.
  4. Add the bay leaves, thyme, paprika or cayenne pepper (if desired), curry powder, salt, and seasoning cubes to the pot. Stir well to combine the spices with the puree.
  5. If using chicken or beef, add it to the pot and cook until it's browned on all sides.
  6. Rinse the rice and add it to the pot, stirring it into the puree until well coated.
  7. Pour in the chicken or vegetable stock, ensuring that the liquid covers the rice by about 1/2 inch (1.25 cm).
  8. Cover the pot and cook over medium-low heat for about 20-30 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  9. If desired, add green peas, carrots, sweet corn, and diced green bell pepper to the pot during the last 10 minutes of cooking. Stir gently to incorporate the vegetables.
  10. Once the rice is cooked and the vegetables are tender, remove the pot from the heat. Allow it to rest for a few minutes before fluffing the rice with a fork.
  11. Your delicious Jollof Rice is now ready to be served!
