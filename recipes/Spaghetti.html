Garnished Jollof Spaghetti

Garnished Jollof Spaghetti: Indulge in a tantalizing fusion of flavors with our Garnished Jollof Spaghetti, where perfectly cooked pasta is infused with a rich tomato-based sauce, vibrant vegetables, and a touch of aromatic spices. Topped with fresh herbs, this dish takes spaghetti to new heights of culinary delight.

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 2 table-spoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup jollof sauce (previously cooked jollof sauce)
  • 1 small onion, freshly chopped
  • 1 small bell pepper, freshly chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or coriander, chopped (for garnish)

Steps in the making:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper and grated carrot to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, curry powder, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Allow the sauce to simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss it with the sauce until well coated.
  6. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley or coriander before serving.
